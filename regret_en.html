A WORLD TOO CONNECTED

Internet and its influence

“Internet” has become a technology that we can no longer do without, whether we like it or not. Today, everything goes through the internet, despite its enormous influence, the internet is extremely young and is no more than 30 years old, it is all the more impressive when today we know that young people spend more than 4 hours of their time on it. Indeed, Generation Z has become extremely dependent on the internet and in particular on social networks and it is not surprising when we know that it was born into it.

Contrary to the other generations who have seen them appearGeneration Z is immersed in technology in their daily lives, they were born with it and cannot live without it. The Internet and social networks are their main communication tool for interacting, whether in private or at work.This generation is permanently connected. She only identifies herself with and through social networks. Much more realistic than their predecessors, for them life and work must be a fluid process, which brings well-being to everyday life.

Image of a phone screen

It gives more importance and is resolutely turned towards new media. The retirement of baby boomers will allow this new generation to find a job more quickly, in better conditions, it is to be wished!
